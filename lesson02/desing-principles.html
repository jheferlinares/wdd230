PARC: Contrast

Netflix

netflix.com

Netflix uses contrast effectively in its user interface design. More than all because of its dark color. For example thumbnails of movies and television shows are displayed on a dark background with high-contrast colored text. This contrast draws the viewer's attention to the content and makes titles and descriptions easier to read.

Visual Hierarchy

Apple

apple.com

Apple's website is a good example of visual hierarchy as they use a combination of size, color and typography to guide users' attention. For example: The main product or message is usually displayed prominently at the top of the page with a large image or bold text, while secondary information is presented in smaller fonts..

White Space and Clean Design

Google

google.com

Google has a clean and minimalist design. Using ample white space on your home page allows the search bar to stand out. There are very few distractions and the user's attention is directed towards the search function. The simplicity and lack of clutter make it easy to use.
